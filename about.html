About Maker Earn

Hi, I'm an indie creator.

Over the past few years, I've tried countless ways to make money online: selling digital products, affiliate marketing, newsletters, freelancing, building tool sites… I've made plenty of mistakes and found a few things that actually work.

Maker Earn is where I organize everything I've learned.

What we cover

  • Digital Products — Gumroad setup, Notion templates, ebook publishing, online courses
  • AI Tools — Latest AI tool reviews, practical tips for AI-assisted creation
  • Side Hustles — Affiliate marketing, Upwork freelancing, Substack monetization
  • Productivity — Project management, automation, essential tool stack for indie creators

Why this site exists

There's too much generic "make money online" advice out there — either too vague ("you need the right mindset") or too outdated (methods from 2018 don't work in 2026).

I built this site to be no-fluff, step-by-step, and immediately actionable. Every article gives you at least one thing you can do right after reading.
